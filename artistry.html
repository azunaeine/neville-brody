Artistry

Brody incorporated dadaism and pop art in his art pieces because they contributed to the growth of his art style. Brody's work contributed to the spread of dadaism and pop art. However, Brody has never copied or replicated pop art and dadaism in his art. He took dadaism and pop art as forms of dynamism and humanism. Brody utilized and tested his self-made san-serifs typography.
